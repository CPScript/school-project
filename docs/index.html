Welcome to SpaceCats

Journey through the cosmos with our intergalactic experience. SpaceCats brings you "out of this world" cuisine that will transport your taste buds to another dimension.

Our Story

Founded in 2025 by a group of cosmic culinary enthusiasts, SpaceCats has been serving the most innovative fusion food from our neon-lit truck. Our mission is to bring the future of food to the present day, combining retro aesthetics with futuristic flavors.

Location & Hours

You can find one of our glowing food trucks at your local curb every weekday from 6:00 PM to 3:00 AM, and on weekends from 6:00 PM until midnight. Follow our social media to stay up to date with special event locations and limited-time menu items.
